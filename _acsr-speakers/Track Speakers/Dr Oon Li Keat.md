<p>Dr Oon was elected to the Membership of Royal College of Psychiatrist
(M.R.C.Psych), UK, in 2015, and received Specialist Accreditation and Registration
in Psychiatry by Singapore Ministry of Health (MOH) and Singapore Medical
Council in 2019. He has been a psychiatrist at the Institute of Mental
Health since 2019, and has worked with adolescents who first presented
with psychosis ranging from age 12 to 17 since 2020.</p>
