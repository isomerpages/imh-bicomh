<p>Dr Xiawei Liu received her Bachelor of Medicine from Guangzhou Medical
University and is currently a graduate student at Central South University,
majoring in psychiatry. She is conducting research on cognition in schizophrenia,
with a particular interest in the early stages of diseases, focusing on
early detection and intervention.</p>
<p>As a resident, Dr Liu primarily engages in patient assessments and assists
in developing treatment plans. She is passionate about clinical work and
psychotherapy.</p>
